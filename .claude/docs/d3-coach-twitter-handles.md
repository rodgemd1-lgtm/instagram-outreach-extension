- Many D3 coaches, especially at smaller programs, do not maintain active Twitter/X accounts

- Carroll University also lists handles on their athletics site for most staff

- UW-River Falls won the 2025 D3 national championship but coaching staff handles are not publicly listed
- For schools marked NOT SEARCHED, web searches did not surface coaching staff or Twitter handles in the results obtained
