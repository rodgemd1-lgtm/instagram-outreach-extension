## Production Pipeline

1. Generate base plates in **Ideogram 2.0** (4 generations per card, pick best)
2. Verify every character for accuracy
3. Import into **Canva Pro** or **CapCut** — add exact stats/URLs as native text
4. Confirm red is exactly #C41E3A
5. Export at 1920x1080 PNG
6. Optional: animate in CapCut (fade-in, light streak, Ken Burns)

- Accent: #C41E3A (Pewaukee red)

## Typography
