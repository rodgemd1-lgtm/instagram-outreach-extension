# Susan Innovation Studio: 2031 Future-Back Lens

## Framing

This plan uses a Strategos-style future-back approach:

1. Define the 2031 destination first.
2. Identify what would need to be true for that destination to exist.
3. Work backward into capability bets, strategic shifts, and reverse milestones.
4. Translate those bets into near-term operating moves.

## 2031 North Star

By 2031, Alex Recruiting should function as an AI-powered recruiting operating system that continuously turns athlete media, coach intelligence, outreach, scheduling, family decisions, and recruiting proof into guided next actions with measurable recruiting outcomes.

The platform should not feel like a dashboard. It should feel like a trusted operating layer:

- it knows what matters now
- it explains why it matters
- it can do the work with approval
- it preserves history and context across years
- it compounds recruiting leverage rather than restarting from zero

## Strategic Shifts

### 1. From dashboard to operating system

The product must move from passive analytics to active orchestration. The user should not have to interpret the data and invent the next move.

### 2. From content engine to recruiting graph

Posts, media, coaches, schools, camps, and reminders cannot remain separate features. They need to become one connected graph of recruiting relevance.

### 3. From manual ops to supervised autonomy

The highest-value version of this product is one where agents prepare, suggest, and execute work inside family-approved guardrails.

### 4. From brochure website to trusted evidence vault

The recruit website should become live proof of the athlete: film, measurables, updates, schedule, character signals, and verified freshness.

## Horizons

### Horizon 1: 2026-2027

Objective: make the system indispensable to one family every week.

Required truths:

- the app always gives a clear next move
- the core rooms are operator-led
- the coach graph is real enough to trust
- the content engine is real enough to publish from
- reminders and workflows reach out proactively

### Horizon 2: 2027-2029

Objective: turn the single-athlete stack into a repeatable recruiting company product.

Required truths:

- multi-athlete architecture exists
- family, trainer, and advisor roles are supported
- recruiting playbooks are reusable across athletes
- the school and coach graph scales
- reporting ties actions to real outcomes

### Horizon 3: 2029-2031

Objective: become the AI recruiting copilot category leader.

Required truths:

- the platform is trusted by families and advisors
- measurable recruiting ROI is visible
- autonomous workflows save real time
- the evidence layer is more current and useful than generic recruiting profiles

## Capability Bets

### Embedded operator

Every major workflow should be executable through chat plus action cards. The UI should explain the room, but the operator should remove the need to hunt through it.

### Recruiting graph intelligence

The platform should know which coaches, schools, and events matter most and what action is likely to move the process forward.

### Verified evidence layer

The athlete website and outbound surfaces should become the canonical proof set for coaches: film, stats, media, links, updates, and freshness.

### AI-assisted family operations

The product should reduce cognitive load for families by routing tasks, reminders, comparisons, deadlines, and follow-up decisions through one operating system.

## Reverse Milestones

### 2026

- stabilize no-dummy-data workflows
- complete operator-led UX in core rooms
- populate real coach, media, and publishing systems

### 2027

- operator can route all primary actions
- reminders, DMs, follows, website updates, and publishing all work end to end

### 2028

- role-based experience for athlete, parent, trainer, and advisor
- strategic planning and approvals become shared workflows

### 2029

- multiple athletes can run on the same product model
- school/coach graph becomes a reusable asset

### 2030

- outcome dashboards connect actions to visits, replies, traction, and commitments

### 2031

- product is recognized as the recruiting operating layer, not just another profile tool
