# Jacob Rodgers Recruit Website 10x Plan

## Aim

Build a coach-first recruiting website that answers the first four questions in order:

1. Who is Jacob?
2. Can he play?
3. Why should I stay interested?
4. What is my next move?

The website should feel like a serious football recruiting packet, not a generic portfolio. It should move like a story, open on identity and film, and then prove projection through development, multi-sport evidence, and a clean contact path.

## Coach-Eye Read

### Why would a coach select Jacob?

- Big-framed 2029 trench prospect with OL/DL flexibility.
- The football clips make it easy to locate `#79`, evaluate first-step urgency, contact point, leverage, and finish.
- Track-and-field performance supports the power story instead of competing with it.
- The site now positions Jacob as a development case with real upside rather than a finished product pretending to be one.

### Why would a coach stay interested?

- The story compounds: football, training, track and field, and snowboarding all reinforce balance, force production, and coordination.
- The page avoids fake certainty. It provides real clips, a real family email, and a clear path to more film.
- The narrative is specific enough to remember: big-framed lineman, power indicators, multi-sport transfer, real work habit.

### What are the moments of truth?

- Get-off
- Strike timing
- Leverage
- Finish
- Effort after first contact
- Whether the page helps a coach find those moments quickly

### Narrative that needs to be said

Jacob Rodgers is a big-framed Pewaukee 2029 trench prospect whose power story is already visible across football film, track-and-field results, and training habits. Coaches should see a player worth projecting, not just a player worth describing.

## Modeled Review Board

### OL/DL coach verdict

`Yay`

The page now gets quickly to the question that matters: what happens when `#79` hits contact? The site makes the rep readable and keeps the strongest clips high on the page.

### Recruiting staff verdict

`Yay`

The public presentation is easier to evaluate and easier to share. The family contact path is clear, the athlete identity is stable, and the full-film links remain available for a deeper pass.

### Story editor verdict

`Yay`

The page has a usable narrative arc: identity, impact reel, athlete development, multi-sport translation, fit, contact.

### Caution

`Nay` only if the site drifts back toward generic claims, weak film ordering, placeholder proof, or fake social proof.

## What Five-Star And High-Momentum Recruits Commonly Do

These patterns are consistently visible across recruiting ecosystems and public profile platforms:

- They make evaluation fast.
- They lead with the strongest plays, not a slow intro.
- They keep profile information consistent across website, film, socials, and recruiting profiles.
- They pair highlight film with deeper film and easy contact.
- They make projection obvious: body type, movement traits, development habits, and role fit.
- They use camps, social visibility, and repeat exposure to stay on staff radar.
- They do not ask coaches to hunt for the story.

- Immediate player identification
- Fast clip turnover
- One idea per clip
- Secondary social-style cuts after the primary coach reel
- Platform adaptation: vertical edits can exist, but the website should present them cleanly in a widescreen frame

## 10x Website Plan

### 1. Coach Mode

- Add a one-click `Coach Mode` that suppresses decorative copy and leaves only measurables, reel, ranked clips, athlete projection, and contact.

### 2. Parent / Family Mode

- Add a softer guidance layer for family sharing with simpler language and fewer evaluation terms.

### 3. Reel System

- Maintain three permanent reel types:
  - Coach-first reel
  - Social/CapCut reel
  - Development reel

### 4. Clip Taxonomy

- Tag every clip by:
  - `OL`
  - `DL`
  - `hands`
  - `leverage`
  - `finish`
  - `pursuit`
  - `training`
  - `track`
  - `snowboard`

### 5. Track And Field Proof

- Add verified throw marks, placements, and school-record proximity as a structured proof block.

### 6. Coach Packet Export

- Generate a downloadable one-page PDF summary with:
  - measurables
  - film links
  - track evidence
  - contact

### 7. AI Clip Assistant

- Build a tagging assistant that recommends:
  - best website clips
  - best X clips
  - best DM clips
  - best camp / coach email clips

### 8. Authenticity Guardrail

- Keep every enhancement honest:
  - no fake stats
  - no altered film speed
  - no invented offers or school interest

### 9. Live Recruiting Layer

- Add real coach interactions and verified activity only when synchronized from trusted data sources.

### 10. Operating Cadence

- Weekly:
  - update the lead clip set
  - update track / football / training notes
  - refresh the coach reel if a clearly better first 20 seconds emerges
