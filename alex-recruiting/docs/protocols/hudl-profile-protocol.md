## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not opting in to recruiting** — Profile is invisible without this
2. **Stale profile** — Most recent content from 6+ months ago signals disinterest
3. **Highlight too long** — 8-10 minutes means nobody watches the whole thing
4. **Wrong plays first** — Putting weakest plays early loses coaches in 30 seconds
5. **Missing contact info** — Coach can't reach you = dead lead
6. **Casual photos** — Selfies, memes, or inappropriate content = immediate pass
7. **No full game film** — Highlights show best plays, full game shows consistency
8. **Ignoring academics** — Missing GPA/transcript raises red flags

---

## Lineman-Specific Tips

- **Show hand placement** — Coaches evaluate technique from film, not just results
- **Include full drives** — 2-3 full drives show consistency and conditioning
- **Show effort plays** — Hustle 20 yards downfield on a screen shows motor
- **Highlight versatility** — DT AND OG reps show coaching flexibility
- **Pre-snap reads** — If possible, show line calls or adjustments at the line
